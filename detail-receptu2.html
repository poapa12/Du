Stuffed Cutlets with Sauerkraut

Delicious cutlets stuffed with sauerkraut, golden-fried bacon, and onions. Prepare roasted slices of potatoes as a side dish and get ready to enjoy this hearty meal that will leave you licking your lips.

Ingredients:

  • 2 onions
  • 750 g potatoes
  • salt
  • ground pepper
  • 100 g English bacon
  • 300 g sauerkraut
  • 1 pinch ground caraway seeds
  • 150 g smoked bacon
  • 1 teaspoon granulated sugar

Instructions:

Cut the smoked bacon into cubes, set aside 50 g, and put the rest into a pot. Finely chop the onion and add it to the pot. Sauté until the bacon releases fat and the onions turn golden brown. Season with caraway seeds and mix. Stir in the sauerkraut with its juice. Season with sugar and salt and simmer over low heat to evaporate the water. Then let it cool. Divide the bone-in cutlet into 5 thick slices. Cut through them to the bone to create pockets. Fill them with sauerkraut and secure with skewers or toothpicks. Fry the remaining chopped bacon in a baking dish. Then place the stuffed cutlets on top. Add about 100 ml of water. Bake in the oven at 170 °C for about 30 minutes. Then increase the temperature to 190 °C and bake for another 15 minutes. Thoroughly wash the potatoes and slice them thinly. Cut the onion into rings, the English bacon into small cubes. Put the bacon in a large skillet and sauté until it releases some fat. Then add the onions with the potatoes, season with salt and pepper, and cook over low heat until everything is tender. Serve the potatoes with the stuffed cutlets.
